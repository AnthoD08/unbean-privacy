🫘 Unbean — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Short version: Unbean stores all your personal data locally on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. The only external service we use is RevenueCat to process subscription payments.

1. Who we are

Unbean is a caffeine-free streak tracking app developed by Anthony Delmotte ("we", "us", or "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at: hello.unbean@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

Unbean is designed with privacy in mind. Here is a complete list of what is stored:

Data stored locally on your device (never sent to our servers):

  • Your caffeine-free streak and check-in history
  • Symptoms you log during check-ins
  • Your settings (notification time, daily cups, price per cup)
  • Your goal (quit or reduce)
  • Your active Bean skin

Data collected by RevenueCat (our payment provider):

  • An anonymous user ID generated automatically (no name or email required)
  • Your subscription status (active, expired, cancelled)
  • Purchase history (for restoring purchases)

3. What data we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location
  • Device contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data sold to third parties

4. How we use your data

The data stored on your device is used exclusively to:

  • Display your streak, history, and progress within the app
  • Send you daily reminders at your chosen time
  • Calculate your savings and show trends

RevenueCat uses your anonymous ID solely to manage your subscription and allow you to restore purchases on a new device.

5. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing. Google Privacy Policy

6. Notifications

Unbean sends local notifications (daily reminders) scheduled directly on your device. These notifications do not require an internet connection and no notification data is sent to external servers.

7. Data retention and deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. There is no account to delete — we hold no data on our servers.

8. Children's privacy

Unbean is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact us
